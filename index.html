Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got clear, honest answers to help you make the best insurance decision.

Is Policy Brothers Insurance registered with IRDAI? +
Yes, Policy Brothers Insurance is an IRDAI-registered insurance advisory firm. We work as licensed advisors with leading insurers across health, life, and motor insurance categories, ensuring all our recommendations and services are fully compliant.
How does Policy Brothers help me choose the right plan? +
Our certified advisors assess your personal situation — age, health, financial goals, and budget — and then compare suitable plans from 25+ insurers. We present you with clear, unbiased recommendations and explain the differences so you can decide with full confidence.
Do I have to pay extra for your advisory services? +
No, our advisory services are completely free. We earn a commission from insurance companies when a policy is sold, so there is no additional charge to you for consultation, comparison, or claims support.
How do I make an insurance claim through Policy Brothers? +
Simply call or WhatsApp us at +91 79821 77480 as soon as an incident occurs. Our claims team will guide you through every step — from submitting documents to tracking the claim — until full settlement. We act as your advocate with the insurer throughout the process.
Which insurance companies do you work with? +
We partner with 25+ IRDAI-approved insurers including HDFC ERGO, Star Health, Niva Bupa, ICICI Lombard, Bajaj Allianz, LIC, HDFC Life, Max Life, Digit Insurance, Acko, New India Assurance, SBI Life, and many more. This ensures you always get the best options in the market.
Can I buy insurance online through Policy Brothers? +
Absolutely. You can get a free quote online, compare plans, and purchase your policy digitally. Our advisors are available via call, WhatsApp, and email to assist you at every step. Policy documents are issued digitally and directly from the insurer to your registered email.
